Taipei

Lively and inviting city, brimming with cultural treasures and fascinating past. Explore Taipei 101 and Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall, then indulge in mouthwatering delights at vibrant night markets. Unwind amidst breathtaking nature in Yangmingshan National Park. Warm-hearted locals, efficient transport, and a harmonious fusion of tradition and contemporary charm await you.
