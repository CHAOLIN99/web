Including meat in one's diet offers several benefits. Firstly, it provides essential nutrients like high-quality proteins, vitamins, and minerals necessary for muscle growth, brain function, and overall health. Secondly, meat contributes to satiety, aiding in weight management and healthy eating habits. Moreover, it meets the specific dietary needs of certain groups and adds cultural and culinary enjoyment to meals. Lastly, responsible meat production supports sustainable practices and local economies. It's important to make informed choices considering individual health, ethics, and the environment, while maintaining a balanced approach to nutrition.
