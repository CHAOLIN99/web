Nantou County

Nestled in the heart of Taiwan, Nantou County is a picturesque paradise that captivates with its natural beauty. Home to the stunning Sun Moon Lake, this county boasts serene waters reflecting the surrounding mountains, creating a breathtaking panorama. Lush green forests and rolling hills paint a vibrant canvas, inviting exploration and tranquility. Nantou's charming villages and tea plantations exude a sense of serenity, offering a glimpse into traditional Taiwanese culture. From hiking trails to hot springs, Nantou County offers a myriad of experiences that immerse visitors in the enchanting wonders of nature.
